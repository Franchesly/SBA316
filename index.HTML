General Plant Care Tips
  • Water your plants as needed; check the soil moisture first.
  • Ensure your plants get the right amount of sunlight for their type.
  • Use a suitable pot with drainage holes to prevent overwatering.
  • Fertilize your plants periodically for healthy growth.
